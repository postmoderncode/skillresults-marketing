About

SkillResults is an innovative software as a service product that allows you to handle your skill and talent management in the cloud. Track employees' current skills, education, training, and proficiencies. Run reports on your gathered data, and help establish training and improvement plans. Define and shape required skills and qualifications for each position to get a solid handle your organizational needs.

Easily deploy to your small-business, department, or enterprise by importing users and sending them secure login links. SkillResults eliminates the need for passwords by using reverse 2-factor authentication. By simply requesting a login on the site, you are emailed a secure login link that is good for a set period of time or until a new one is requested. Your login is secure because only you can access your work email account, just be sure not to forward the message to anyone else.

Quit guessing at your organization's current skill set and proficiencies. Make talent acquisition and management a simplified part of your overall strategy. Identify and leverage the potential that you already have while fostering development and defining and guiding toward future need.

No more shadow systems and floating spreadsheets. No more scrambling to identify the skills and responsibilities for an organizational position. With SkillResults, you are in control of the talent within your organization, and you can help define and drive individual and collective skills and training.

Let's be honest, you don't have time to build a system from the ground up, nor do you want to waste weeks filling it with data. That's why SkillResults has done the heavy lifting for you. Our skill catalog has broken down skills by Area and Category to present an intuitive taxonomy of personal and organizational skills.

You can easily customize the skill catalog for your organization. SkillResults gives administrators complete control over Areas, Categories, and individual Skills. Organizational users can add their skills and competencies from the catalog, or by our powerful search tool that filters through the available skills as they type. Users select a skill with a click, and add their proficiency rating with the skill. They can also add ratings of where they'd like to be on their Skill Wishlist.

SkillResults makes recording education credentials a breeze with a simple interface that has over 9000 institutions already pre-loaded and ready to search by state. We also have most major and minor fields of study, popular certifications, and licensing. Now you won't have to guess who is credentialed in what because you'll have that information, along with skills, at your fingertips.

Reliable, Available, Redundant, and Secure. Let SkillResults handle your cloud skill integration. SkillResults is run on Microsoft Azure and utilizes Microsoft Azure SQL Server to provide enterprise level service whenever and wherever you need it. Future integration efforts will bring the option of auto-authenticating with your work credentials for all Office365 users, in addition to our already available secure passwordless login system.
