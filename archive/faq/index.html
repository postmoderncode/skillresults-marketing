FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions, a quick reference to the various aspects of SkillResults

What is SkillResults?

SkillResults is an online system designed to help keep track of the skills and education of your organization's employees.

How does it work?

Simply sign up, and import your users by their email address (individually or .csv). You can send them automated welcome emails with secure login links, or invite them to participate yourself in which case they can simply login with their email address.

Adding skills is easy using the pre-populated skill catalog. Simply select a skill, set your proficiency, and add it to your profile. Our skill catalog makes it easy to discover skills broken out by area and category, or you can simply search for them by name.

What else can it do?

Besides skill tracking SkillResults can also help track certifications, licenses, and degrees using the education section. Already loaded with over 9000 institutions of higher learning and vocational schools, as well as known award types and fields of study, adding and tracking education has never been easier.

There's also the Skill Wishlist where your organization's users can add in the skills that they want to learn or the existing talents that they want to develop further.

We are constantly adding new features, so refer to our development roadmap to see what SkillResults can do next!

How does that help me?

Building a cross-disciplinary team and like to know who's good at what? Implementing a training or development plan and want to track progress? Want to define the roles in your organization by required skills and credentials? Ever wonder what the people you work with wish they were better at?

Login with email address? You mean no passwords?

Why, do you need another one to remember? There's no need for a passwords because you interact with SkillResults using your most secure channel already, your work email. You simply enter your organizational email address into SkillResults and you are immediately sent a secure login link. That unique hyperlink takes you to, and authenticates you on, SkillResults. One click and you are sitting at your dashboard already logged in.

It's like 2-factor authentication in reverse, we don't bother you with arbitrary passphrases and instead go right for the highest security validation available, your personal work inbox. By sending a unique secure login link directly to you, it gives you a safe and convenient one-click access.

Secure Login Link?

That's right. Secured by it's uniqueness to you as a user, and sent to your already secured work inbox, this hyperlink brings you right into SkillResults already logged in. Goodbye password resets, and rules, and having to memorize another string of nonsense. When a registered user enters their email address and clicks login, SkillResults generates a unique set of keys that will allow that user access, and then emails them as a big shiny button.

You can reuse the link to access SkillResults until you request a new one from the login page, or until a set period of time expires. Your personal key to the system is in your hands, so don't share your email. Although, if you did accidently leave your key out, changing the locks is as easy as logging in again and generating a new key.

How was it built?

Things are about to get technical, so if you aren't into that sort of thing, turn back now. SkillResults was built with AngularJS by Google, and ASP.NET Web API by Microsoft. AngularJS handles all of the user interface component such as templating, routing, and data binding. Web API handles the RESTful interface between the data context and the UI. The backend SQL database and Web API endpoints are hosted on Microsoft Azure with the endpoints secured using Token Based Authentication.
