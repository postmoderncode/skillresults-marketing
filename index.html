CASE #1 - Private or Public Companies

A progressive company with diverse business operation has implemented a new strategic initiative to take inventory of in house talent, forecast needed skills for future business roles, and implement employee training and development plans including online coursework, and cross-disciplinary teams.

With SkillResults they easily delegated capturing and populating this data right to the employees. It cost them zero headaches or favors from IT as secure login links to the cloud system are delivered to their work inbox on demand. Now they can easily run reports on who knows what, what their organizational skill concentrations and weak areas are, desired skill improvement from the employees, and build strong teams with the right mix of talent to succeed while cross-training.

Employee development plans are now a breeze with skills, proficiencies, and desired improvement areas clearly spelled out right from the source. Purchasing online training for skill development have become a targeted science instead of throwing money at the wall and hoping something sticks, and the positive results of increased communication personal relationship with the workforce really shows.

CASE #2 - Educational Institutions

For example, a Private Institution of Higher Learning has an extensive online adult-education program and currently contracts over 1000 adjunct instructors. It has a difficult time keeping track of who is both credentialed and qualified to teach various courses, as well as keeping records of who has completed required training for their LMS (Learning Management System) and other internal systems and policies.

Using SkillResults, the university has an easy to access and manage skill matrix of all of their available adjuncts. They have even expanded use to full-time faculty for tenure and promotion, as well as staff to help establish development plans and annual review.

FAQs
What is SkillResults?

SkillResults is an online system designed to help keep track of the skills and education of your organization's employees.

How does it work?

Simply sign up, and import your users by their email address (individually or .csv). You can send them automated welcome emails with secure login links, or invite them to participate yourself in which case they can simply login with their email address.

Adding skills is easy using the pre-populated skill catalog. Simply select a skill, set your proficiency, and add it to your profile. Our skill catalog makes it easy to discover skills broken out by area and category, or you can simply search for them by name.

What else can it do?

Besides skill tracking SkillResults can also help track certifications, licenses, and degrees using the education section. Already loaded with over 9000 institutions of higher learning and vocational schools, as well as known award types and fields of study, adding and tracking education has never been easier.

There's also the Skill Wishlist where your organization's users can add in the skills that they want to learn or the existing talents that they want to develop further.

Login with email address? You mean no passwords?

SkillResults uses a secure email address based, login token system. There's no need for a passwords because you interact with SkillResults using your most secure channel already, your work email. You simply enter your organizational email address into SkillResults and you are immediately sent a secure login link. That unique hyperlink takes you to, and authenticates you on, SkillResults. One click and you are sitting at your dashboard already logged in.

It's like 2-factor authentication in reverse, we don't bother you with arbitrary passphrases and instead go right for the highest security validation available, your personal work inbox. By sending a unique secure login link directly to you, it gives you safe and convenient one-click access.

How does the login link work?

When you setup an employee in SkillResults, they are sent an automatic login link to their inbox. If they would like to login later, they just click "Login" on SkillResults.com and they are sent a brand new login link. Goodbye password resets, and complex login systems. No more having to memorize another string of nonsense. When a registered user enters their email address and clicks login, SkillResults generates a unique set of keys that will allow that user access.

You can reuse the link to access SkillResults until you request a new one from the login page, or until a set period of time expires. Your personal key to the system is in your hands, so don't share your email. Although, if you did accidently leave your key out, changing the locks is as easy as logging in again and generating a new key.
